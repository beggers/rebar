Summary:
- The project started as an agent-operated regex-parser effort aimed at
  eventually surpassing CPython.
- It evolved into a Rust-backed, CPython-facing, bug-for-bug compatible drop-in
  replacement for Python's `re` module.
- The operating model centered on a supervisor that owns indefinite progress
  and may change the harness, prompts, config, and active agents.
- Human-readable reporting in `README.md` became a first-class requirement,
  with future correctness and benchmark scorecards wired into the reporting
  path.
